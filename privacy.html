Privacy Policy

BigBrain Prompting is a local-first iPhone app for building, saving, filling, copying, and exporting AI prompts. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information We Collect

BigBrain Prompting does not collect personal information, create user accounts, use analytics SDKs, track you across apps or websites, or send your prompt content to our servers.

Prompt Drafts and Saved Prompts

Prompt drafts, saved prompts, favorites, categories, variable values, and app preferences are stored locally on your device. They remain on your device unless you choose to copy, share, export, back up, or otherwise move them using iOS features.

No AI Provider Connection

The app does not connect to OpenAI, Anthropic, or any other AI provider. If you copy or export a prompt and paste it into another app or website, that separate service handles the content under its own privacy policy.

Diagnostics

Apple may provide crash reports or diagnostics to developers if you have enabled that sharing in iOS settings. Those reports are handled by Apple and are used only to improve app reliability.

Website Hosting

This website may be hosted by GitHub Pages. GitHub may process standard server logs and technical information needed to operate the website. BigBrain Prompting does not use this website to collect app prompt content.

Children's Privacy

BigBrain Prompting is not designed to collect information from children. Because the app does not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, open a support request at github.com/Shailey27/BigBrain/issues.
