Common Questions

Does BigBrain Prompting send my prompts to an AI model?

No. The app helps you draft, save, fill, copy, and export prompts. It does not send prompts to any AI provider.

Where are my saved prompts stored?

Saved prompts are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage.

Can I export a prompt?

Yes. Use the Markdown tab to copy or export the generated prompt as a Markdown file.

How do prompt variables work?

Saved prompts can include placeholders like {{topic}} or {{audience}}. The Library tab detects those variables and gives you fields to fill before copying.
